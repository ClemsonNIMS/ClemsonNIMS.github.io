Our Mission

NIMS seeks to connect students with resources they might not be aware of. Clemson has so many amazing groups, clubs, shops and opportunities that it's hard to keep track of them all. That's where we come in; your one-stop-shop for all things making, learning and playing at Clemson.

We also provide services for student organizations and clubs. We want to connect people with a passion to create and use this energy to drive improvements that benefit everyone. NIMS aims to champion student groups and leverage the Network to get dedicated space, resources and funding. If two heads are better than one, we're shooting for 2000.
